About Me

I'm a High Performance Computing (HPC) scientist in the Co-Design Team at Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL), and a member of both the LANL AI Strike Force and the ISO C++ Standards Committee. My research focuses on exploring emerging parallel and distributed architectures to enable the next generation of supercomputers for scientific simulation.

I work across several key HPC and AI projects, including topology development in FleCSI, GPU acceleration with Kokkos in the ExaShEDS project, and multi-physics modeling as the lead computer science developer for FleCSPH, a Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics framework.

I'm also the lead of the Co-Design Summer School at LANL, a 10-week immersive program where six students work collaboratively using a co-design approach to tackle a shared scientific computing challenge. The school emphasizes teamwork, hands-on development, and exposure to cutting-edge HPC and AI tools.

Areas of expertise: HPC, C++, accelerator programming (GPGPU), performance optimization, large-scale multi-physics simulations, and AI for science.
Current projects: FleCSI, FleCSI-SP, FleCSPH, ExaShEDS, AI Strike Force.

Hobbies & Interests

Outside of HPC and research, I enjoy a range of hands-on, creative, and outdoor activities.

  • Wildlife Photography: I love capturing nature through my lens. Most of my photos end up on Instagram, when I find the time to sort through them!
  • Woodworking: I spend a lot of time in the workshop making furniture. I’m especially fascinated by Japanese joinery and am working to improve my skills in that area. I might even share some of my projects here.
  • Japan: I have a deep love for Japanese culture and am learning the language with the goal of attending woodworking classes there someday. I’m also dreaming about buying an Akiya (abandoned house), eventually!
  • Hiking: I enjoy getting outside whenever I can, especially with my dog, Louis. Nature is my recharge button.
  • Music & Art: I play the trumpet and have performed in several bands in both France and the U.S. I also enjoy painting, whether it's with oils or miniatures like Warhammer 40K.
